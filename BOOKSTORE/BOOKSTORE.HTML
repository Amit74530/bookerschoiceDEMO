Our Story

Dehradun’s most loved local bookstores. Thought to have started in the late 1980s or early 1990s, the store began as a modest bookstall and gradually transformed into a vibrant literary hub, trusted by generations of readers. What sets us apart? We offer a rare blend of new and secondhand books, including fiction, non-fiction, encyclopedias, religious texts, and even popular manga titles like Naruto and Spy x Family. With prices often ranging from just ₹150–₹200, we make reading affordable and accessible for everyone. Unlike corporate bookshops, Reader’s Choice is driven by warmth, honesty, and personal connection. Visitors often praise the friendly and helpful owner, and many return not just for the books, but for the experience. We’ve earned our place in the community by being different from the rest—offering unique finds, fair prices, and a welcoming vibe. Whether you're looking for a bestseller, a hidden gem, or a nostalgic reread, Reader’s Choice is a place where stories live on, and feel at home.
